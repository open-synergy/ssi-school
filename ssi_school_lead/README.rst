Extends CRM Lead with school-specific fields:

- **School**: links the lead to a ``school`` record
- **Student Name**: name of the prospective student
- **Student Birth Date**: date of birth of the prospective student

These fields allow PPDB (school admission) leads coming from the website
Contact Us form to be enriched with school and student information.
